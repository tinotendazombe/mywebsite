About the Podcast

Welcome to Cash & Chaos, the podcast that explores the intersection of entrepreneurship, side hustles, and the unpredictable journey of making money in today's world.

Each episode dives deep into real stories from the trenches - the wins, the fails, and the unpredictable chaos that comes with chasing financial freedom.

Whether you're a seasoned entrepreneur or just starting your hustle, we bring the insights, inspiration, and straight talk you need to navigate the money game.
